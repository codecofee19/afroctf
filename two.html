When you browse the internet, every website you have visit has an address. It looks like a bunch of random numbers like 154.13.415.431

These numbers are called IP Addresses or internet protocol addresses. Kind of like a home address but for computers.

Your computer converts domain names like google.com and facebook.com to these special numbers because facebook.com is ALOT easier to remember than 151.31.415.51
