From Stack Overflow 2018 Demographics Survey

As you can see, these are alarmingly low numbers compared to the general population where

  • 50.4% of general population are women
  • 12% of general population are LGBTQ
  • 13.3% of general population are black or African American
From US Consensus 2016 & GLAAD Accelerating Acceptance 2017 Report
